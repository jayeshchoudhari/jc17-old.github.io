Short Bio
I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science department, IIT Gandhinagar since December 2014. I am privileged to work under the supervision of Prof. Anirban Dasgupta .
Interests
There are number of things I am curious about, but currently, my research focus is Algorithms for large data, Social Networks, and Parameterized Algorithms. In particular, I am working in understanding the dynamics of information diffusion in social networks, estimating some basic properties of large graph efficiently, and parameterized algorithms for some hard graph problems.
